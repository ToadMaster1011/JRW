About This Property

Welcome to Lakeview Retreat — a beautifully renovated 3-bedroom home nestled in the peaceful Sunset Hills neighbourhood. Whether you're planning a family vacation, a romantic getaway, or simply need a quiet place to work remotely, this property delivers comfort and style in equal measure.

The open-plan living and dining area is bathed in natural light and flows seamlessly to the large private backyard — perfect for morning coffees or evening BBQs. The fully equipped modern kitchen has everything you need to cook at home, and the spacious bedrooms each feature plush bedding and quality linens for a great night's sleep.

The master bedroom comes with an ensuite bathroom and king-size bed, while the other two rooms offer a queen and twin arrangement. A second full bathroom serves the rest of the home.
